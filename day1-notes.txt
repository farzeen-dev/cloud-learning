Day 1: Introduction to Cloud Computing

1. What is cloud computing?
Cloud computing allows users to store and access data and computing resources over the internet without needing physical infrastructure.

2. Examples of cloud services I use:
- Gmail
- Google Drive
- YouTube

3. Why companies use cloud computing:
- Flexible and accessible
- Cost-effective
- Easy to scale and manage
- Offers high-level security
